Classes & timetable

We teach four core disciplines with dedicated kids’ sessions, open mat time, and private tuition slots throughout the week.

Martial arts we teach

Each art is taught with clear fundamentals and safe, progressive sparring where appropriate.

Jiu-jitsu

Brazilian jiu-jitsu–style grappling: positions, submissions, and rolling for all levels.

Karate

Striking, kata, and discipline drawn from traditional karate practice.

Judo

Throws, grip fighting, and ne-waza with a strong emphasis on ukemi and control.

Muay Thai

Thai boxing technique — pads, bags, and controlled partner drills.
